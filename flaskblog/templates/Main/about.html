About Flask Blog

Hello everyone!! This is a small blogging website where you can create posts. All you have to do is to create an account and log in. Don't worry if there are some typos in your post, you can always update or delete them later. You could also view posts written by others. This website also offers you an option to change your profile photo as per your personal preferences. If you forgot you password, we'll send an email to your registered Email-ID with a link to reset your password. Still reading this section? Go ahead and register to experience all of the above features in action!!
PS. More features to be addded soon. You could send some suggestions to my LinkedIn Profile.
Also, one last thing, since I have deployed this project on Heroku for free, any data created is lost in an hour, since it follows a Ephemeral filesystem
